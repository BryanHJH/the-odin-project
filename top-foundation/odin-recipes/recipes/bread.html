Bread Recipe

Description

Average Preparation Time: 5 minutes

Average Cooking Time: 3 hours

Total time taken: 3 hours 5 minutes

Serving size: 12 pax

Yield: 1 - 1 1/2 pound loaf

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cupps bread flour
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast

Steps

  1. Place water, sugar, salt, oil, bread flour and yeast into pan of bread machine

  2. Bake oin White Bread setting. Cool on wire racks before slicing.
